# 🛡️ StockSentinel — Premium AI Trading Intelligence

StockSentinel is an advanced, high-fidelity AI-powered financial intelligence terminal. It integrates real-time asset feeds, neural technical indicator analysis, and natural language Gemini reasoning to empower investors and traders with deep market wisdom.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🔍 1. Smart Stock Search
StockSentinel allows you to search and select any stock of your choice based on the company name or stock code. Upon selection, the application fetches real-time and historical market data from Yahoo Finance.
* **Search Anywhere**: Find assets instantly by company name or ticker code (e.g., AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, BTC, ETH).
* **Real-Time Data**: Stream instant prices, volumes, and market configurations.
* **OHLC Metrics**: View precise Open, High, Low, Close (OHLC) metrics.
* **Historical Series**: View interactive financial tracking graphs across multiple stepping intervals.

---

### 📊 2. Technical Analysis Engine
The application analyzes the selected stock using technical indicators popular among seasoned investors and traders. It translates raw mathematical values into simple, actionable interpretations:
* **Relative Strength Index (RSI)**: Identifies oversold (<30) or overbought (>70) momentum.
* **MACD Oscillator**: Gauges short-term vs long-term exponential trends for bullish/bearish crossovers.
* **Moving Averages (50 & 200 Days)**: Computes Golden and Death crosses to capture structural macro trends.
* **Volume Trend Validation**: Validates the strength and institutional backing of price actions.

---

### 🤖 3. Explainable AI Recommendation Engine
Unlike simple buy/sell signal models, StockSentinel details the exact multi-horizon vectors influencing each decision, giving you confidence and clear reasoning:
* **Strategic Signal**: Strong Buy, Buy, Hold, Sell, or Strong Sell.
* **Confidence Scale**: Precision score indicating prediction strength.
* **Price Milestones**: Dynamic price targets projected for tomorrow, 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months.
* **Verifiable Reasons**: Bulleted breakdowns of technical catalysts.

---

### 📰 4. AI-Powered News Sentiment Analysis
StockSentinel scans the global financial web, extracts major press and news releases for the selected asset, and generates real-time sentiment metrics:
* **Overall Sentiment**: Categorized into Positive, Neutral, or Negative.
* **Algorithmic Summarization**: Concise bullet points highlighting the catalysts (e.g., strong earnings, strategic partnerships, analyst outlooks).
* **Impact Scores**: Visual indicators of the news weight relative to price impact.

---

### ⚖️ 5. Intelligent Stock Comparison
Formulate side-by-side strategic battles between any two assets. Compare technical frameworks, relative momentum, and AI-driven growth projections:
* **RSI Correlation**: Side-by-side comparison of momentum extremes.
* **MACD Convergence**: Parallel analysis of signal trends.
* **Advantage Badges**: Automatically displays which asset carries the better momentum, lower risk, or higher confidence.
* **Growth Comparatives**: Side-by-side percentage growth estimates.

---

### 🎓 6. Learning Mode (StockSentinel Academy)
StockSentinel doesn't just display calculations; it empowers your financial literacy. Learn the concepts behind technical indicators through clear, interactive modules:
* **Academic Definitions**: Clear, jargon-free explanations of complex oscillators.
* **Implication Analysis**: Detailed instructions on how calculated values affect live price ranges.
* **Real-World Analogies**: Fun and intuitive physical analogies to make abstract indicators stick.

---

## ⭐ Unique Innovation — Bull vs Bear AI Debate

StockSentinel pioneers a balanced approach to market decision-making with its unique **Bull vs Bear AI Debate Engine**. Instead of displaying a single, biased signal, StockSentinel splits its Gemini reasoning engine into two adversarial roles to debate both sides of the coin:

### 🟢 Bull AI (The Optimist)
* **Healthy RSI Support**: Validates buying zones when momentum is stable.
* **MACD Bullish Crossover**: Tracks trend acceleration.
* **Positive Catalyst Highlights**: Identifies bullish volume expansion and accumulation.

### 🔴 Bear AI (The Realist)
* **Resistance Walls**: Warns you of major sell barriers and psychological resistance levels.
* **Market Volatility Risks**: Evaluates macroeconomic noise and general index pressure.
* **Valuation Multiples**: Keeps speculation in check by monitoring overbought risks.

### ⚖️ Final AI Verdict Synthesis
* **Weighted Reconciliations**: Weighs the bullish thesis against the risk factors to deliver a final recommendation with an explainable, balanced report card.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Requirements
* **Frontend**: React 18 with TypeScript & Vite
* **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
* **Animations**: Motion (f.k.a. Framer Motion)
* **Charts**: Recharts & D3
* **AI Model**: Google Gemini 3.5 Flash
* **State Management**: React Hooks with local persistence fallback
